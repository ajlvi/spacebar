In SpaceBar, you use five-letter words to gather information about a secret six-letter word.

Your five-letter guesses will have a space in them. You can position that space wherever you'd like.. Hit Enter or press the ⏎ button to register your guess.

When you think you have the secret word figured out, guess it. But beware—an incorrect six-letter guess only tells you that it's wrong!

Example output:

S
P
—
A
R
K

This means the letter S is in the secret word, though it is not first; the fourth letter of the secret word is A; and the letters K, P, and R are not in the secret word.
